# 💫 About Me:

I graduated from Providence College of Engineering with a BTech in Computer Science, where I learned the fundamentals of programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering. I have more than one year of professional experience in web development, and I am passionate about creating user-friendly, secure, and scalable solutions that enhance learning outcomes and empower educators. My core competencies include web development, database management, Blockchain, problem-solving, and teamwork. I am always eager to learn new skills and technologies, and to collaborate with other developers and stakeholders to deliver high-quality products that meet the needs and expectations of the clients.
